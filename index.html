Welcome to Trini Kitchen

Savor the flavors of Trinidad with our BBQ & Jerk Chicken, Fried Shark, Grilled Salmon, and more. We also offer catering for all events! Visit us in Queens!

Menu Highlights

  • BBQ & Jerk Chicken: Smoky, spicy, and full of flavor.
  • Fried Shark: A Trinidadian classic, perfectly crispy.
  • Grilled Salmon: Fresh and flavorful, grilled to perfection.
  • 6 Flavors Wings: Classic BBQ, Rasta Jerk, Soca (Pineapple Sweet Chili), Chutney (Mango Habanero), Tobago (Lemon Pepper), Moruga (Really Spicy).
  • Sides: Macaroni Pie (Trini Mac n Cheese), French Fries, Fried Rice, Mashed Potatoes, Macaroni Salad (Cold), Cole Slaw.
  • Rasta Pasta Entrees: Pair with any meat or wings.
